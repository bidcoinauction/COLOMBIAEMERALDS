A Legacy of Excellence

Uniting generations of emerald mastery with a modern vision to bring the world's finest emeralds to Canada.

Tradition Meets Innovation

At Canadian Emeralds, we unite the heritage of world-class emerald expertise with the future of innovation. Naftalia Gad, a third-generation member of the renowned Gad family, partners with Alistair Ewart, a Canadian entrepreneur and leader in artificial intelligence.

This collaboration blends generations of emerald mastery with modern vision, creating an unprecedented gateway for Canadians to experience, trust, and invest in the finest emeralds the world has to offer.

The Gad Family Legacy

As the third generation of a family deeply rooted in the emerald trade, Naftalia carries forward a legacy built through his father and grandfather, who established the Gad family as one of the premier global sources of fine Colombian emeralds. With direct ownership and partnerships in Colombia’s most celebrated mines, the Gad family has unrivaled access to stones of extraordinary quality and provenance. The Gad name has become synonymous with authenticity, excellence, and ethical sourcing—values that Naftalia continues to champion.

A Vision for the Future

As a founder of Koat.ai, Alistair has already proven himself a global leader in AI and data innovation, building platforms that power decision-making for high-profile clients within competitive industries. His expertise in leveraging technology and data-driven strategy ensures that Canadian Emeralds not only brings the world’s finest emeralds to Canada but also introduces them to the market in a modern, intelligent, and impactful way.
