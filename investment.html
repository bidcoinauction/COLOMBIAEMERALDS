WHY EMERALDS OUTPERFORM

Geological Scarcity

Emeralds are 30 times rarer than diamonds, with only three major producing regions worldwide. Colombian emeralds represent less than 1% of global gemstone production.

Consistent Appreciation

High-quality emeralds have appreciated at 5-8% annually since 1995, significantly outperforming inflation and many traditional investments.

Market Stability

Unlike stocks or bonds, emeralds show low volatility and maintain value during economic uncertainty, serving as an effective hedge against market downturns.

MARKET TRENDS & OUTLOOK

Growing Demand

Increasing global demand for colored gemstones, particularly in Asia-Pacific markets, driving sustained price growth for premium specimens.

Supply Constraints

Limited mining capacity and increasing environmental regulations restricting new emerald production, creating favorable supply-demand dynamics.

Institutional Interest

Growing institutional and high-net-worth investor interest in alternative assets, with emeralds becoming a preferred portfolio diversification tool.
